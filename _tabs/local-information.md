## Cebu

**Cebu** is an island province located in the heart of the **Philippines**.  Beyond its dynamic urban landscape, Cebu offers a rich blend of natural and cultural attractions. The island is home to breathtaking waterfalls, freshwater springs, and scenic mountain landscapes – ideal for those seeking respite from city life. Its coastline is dotted with white-sand beaches, world-class resorts, and charming islets. Equally memorable is the warmth and hospitality of the Cebuano people, who welcome visitors with vibrant local culture and a diverse array of Filipino cuisine and delicacies.

## Language

Filipinos are known to be multilingual and fluent English speakers.  **Cebuano**, **Filipino**, and **English** are mainly used in Cebu.

## Electricity

The power sockets are of **type A (USA)**. The standard voltage is **220V** and the standard frequency is **60 Hz**.

## Weather

The Philippines is situated in the tropical zone and has 2 seasons, the wet or the rainy season and the dry or the summer season.  November is the end of the rainy season, and the temperature is between **22 and 29 degrees Celsius**.

## Local Transportation

Mactan Island is accessible via air through the Mactan–Cebu International Airport, which is located in Lapu-Lapu City (30 minutes away from Cebu City).  Upon arrival, the following are available transportation options to Cebu City:

- Standard taxis
- Premium taxis
- Buses
- Car rental services (we recommend getting a quotation prior to your trip)
- Ride-sharing applications (e.g., [Grab](https://www.grab.com/ph/), [InDrive](https://indrive.com/), [Green GSM](https://www.greengsm.ph/en))
- Plantation Bay Resort and Spa also offers shuttle service
